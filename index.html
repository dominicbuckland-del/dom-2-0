What kind of clients do you work with?

Operators and founder-led businesses where the work needs to ship. Typical engagements are Australian SMEs — logistics, construction, property, nonprofit — and product teams who need a senior operator without the agency overhead.

What services do you offer?

Marketing systems, custom software, brand & positioning, and operations automation. Most engagements cross disciplines — which is usually where the leverage lives.

How do you price your projects?

Scope, timeline, and deliverables. Never by the hour. After a discovery call I send a fixed-scope proposal. Monthly partnerships for continuous delivery; 90-day sprints for scoped outcomes.

What is your typical timeline?

Branding sprints 4–6 weeks; software sprints 8–12 weeks; monthly partnerships are ongoing. Milestones agreed before kickoff.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Based in Brisbane, working with clients across Australia and globally. Weekly calls, shared working docs, and direct access by Slack or email keep things tight.

Do you take on one-off tasks?

I prefer full-scope engagements for cohesion. Smaller, well-defined pieces are possible when they fit the bench. Reach out and I’ll tell you straight.

How many revisions are included?

One strong direction, refined through structured feedback. The number of revision rounds depends on scope, but clarity and alignment are the priority from the start.

What is an AI-native studio?

A practice built from the ground up around AI tools and workflows — not an agency retrofitting AI into its existing model. It means one operator using AI as a force multiplier to produce the output of a larger team: faster, fewer handoffs, lower cost.
